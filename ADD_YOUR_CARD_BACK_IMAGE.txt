🎨 IMAGE TIPS:

Best Results:
  ✓ PNG format with transparency
  ✓ Square dimensions (512x512 or 1024x1024)
  ✓ Blue color scheme (matches poker theme)
  ✓ Clean, geometric design
  ✓ High contrast for visibility

Avoid:
  ✗ JPG format (no transparency)
  ✗ Very small images (will look pixelated)
  ✗ Complex gradients (may not show well at small size)
  ✗ Text that's too small to read
