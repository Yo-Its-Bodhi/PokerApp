🎮 HOW TO PLAY


Step 1: Start Demo Mode
────────────────────────

  1. Open http://localhost:5177
  2. Click "DEMO MODE" button
  3. Click "JACK IN" to sit at seat 1 or 4
  4. Enter your name and choose avatar
  5. Click "START DEMO HAND"


Step 2: Play the Hand
─────────────────────

  The game will:
    → Post blinds automatically (SB: 500, BB: 1000)
    → Deal 2 hole cards to you (face-up)
    → Deal 2 hole cards to AI (face-down)
    → Start with preflop betting

  Your turn (timer starts):
    → Choose: FOLD, CHECK, CALL, RAISE, or ALL IN
    → Your action appears in game log

  AI's turn (1.5s delay):
    → AI makes decision based on bet size
    → AI action appears in game log


Step 3: Watch Cards Reveal
───────────────────────────

  After betting round completes:

    FLOP: 
      → 3 cards flip from back to front
      → 0.15s delay between each card
      → New betting round begins

    TURN:
      → 4th card flips over
      → New betting round

    RIVER:
      → 5th card flips over
      → Final betting round

    SHOWDOWN:
      → AI cards reveal
      → Winner determined
      → Pot awarded


Step 4: Next Hand
─────────────────

  After 3 seconds:
    → New hand starts automatically
    → Dealer button rotates
    → Blinds post
    → New cards dealt
    → Continue playing!

💡 GAME LOGIC DETAILS


Blind Structure:
  • Small Blind: 500 SHIDO
  • Big Blind: 1,000 SHIDO
  • In heads-up: Dealer posts SB

Starting Stacks:
  • You: 100,000 SHIDO
  • AI Opponent: 100,000 SHIDO

AI Decision Making:
  • If you raise:
    - 20% fold
    - 50% call
    - 30% re-raise

  • If you check:
    - 60% check
    - 40% bet

Betting Rounds:
  1. PREFLOP: After hole cards dealt
  2. FLOP: After 3 community cards
  3. TURN: After 4th community card
  4. RIVER: After 5th community card
  5. SHOWDOWN: Reveal and determine winner

Hand Restart:
  • Winner gets pot added to stack
  • New hand starts after 3 seconds
  • Dealer button rotates
  • Blinds auto-posted
